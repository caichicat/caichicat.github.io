The History of VR

The first VR

The first VR machine was created by Morton Heilig and patented in 1962. Unlike the ones we know of today, the VR machine allowed not only visual effects but also smell, vibrations, and atmospheric effects such as wind.

Types of VR

1980-2000s

Jaron Lanier and Thomas Zimmerman created a headset and goggles which would help lead the design what we know of today. Many years later, scientists would study this field and make progress on it.
